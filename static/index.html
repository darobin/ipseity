Interplanetary Standards

The purpose of Interplanetary Standards is to foster interoperability between independent implementations of parts of the interplanetary stack (IPFS, IPLD, libp2p, Multiformats) by producing:

  • High-quality open standards that fully defined implementation behaviour and empower anyone to build their own system that fits into our broader ecosystem without needing to reverse engineer the behaviour of other parts.
  • Comprehensive test suites that offer guarantees of correctness and are kept updated as issues with implementations arise.
  • Testing infrastructure to support detecting interoperability issues in CI/CD and to report on implementation maturity as the stack evolves.
  • A governance model that guarantees community ownership of the standards and the standards-production process, in pursuit of efficient, transformative, ethical technology.

The specifications are broken up into multiple areas that cover the entirety of the stack. Each area may have different governance arrangements so as to ensure that it runs in the way that is the most appropriate for that community, but all must follow the governance set for the overall project.
